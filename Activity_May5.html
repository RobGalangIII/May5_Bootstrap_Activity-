Jujutsu Kaisen Characters

Explore some of the most powerful and intriguing figures from the series.

Gojo Satoru

The strongest jujutsu sorcerer, known for his Six Eyes and Limitless technique. A powerful, confident teacher aiming to reform the jujutsu world.

Sukuna

Also known as Ryomen Sukuna, he’s a legendary cursed spirit called the “King of Curses.” Sadistic and powerful, residing within Yuji Itadori.

Toji Fushiguro

A feared assassin with no cursed energy, known as the “Sorcerer Killer.” Relies on physical prowess and cursed tools; father of Megumi Fushiguro.
